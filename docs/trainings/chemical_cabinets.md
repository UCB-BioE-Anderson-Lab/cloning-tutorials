# Chemical Cabinet Access Training

This training applies to anyone who wants to access chemicals stored in the chemical cabinet in **B144 (Stanley Hall)**.

## Before you access the cabinet

You must:

1. Read the SOP below in full.
2. Read the Safety Data Sheet (SDS) for any chemical you plan to use.
3. Sign the printed training acknowledgment posted on the **safety board** in the safety corner of **B144**.
4. Ask lab personnel if you are unsure whether your planned use is covered.

Do **not** access the cabinet until you have completed all of the above.

## Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)

This cabinet is governed by the lab SOP for flammable liquids and solids:

- 📄 Flammable Liquids and Solids SOP: [Download PDF](../assets/flammable_liquids_and_solids.pdf)

This SOP includes requirements for:

- hazard awareness and review of the relevant SDS before use
- required engineering controls, including use of a fume hood when applicable
- required PPE
- safe handling and storage
- spill response, first aid, and waste disposal
- documented training before use

## Training acknowledgment requirement

To be authorized to access the cabinet, you must sign the physical training acknowledgment page posted on the **safety board** in **B144** after reading the SOP.

Your signature confirms that you:

- have read and understood the SOP
- understand the hazards of the chemicals you plan to use
- know the required PPE, handling, storage, spill, and waste procedures
- will review the relevant SDS before use
- will ask for help before proceeding if anything is unclear
